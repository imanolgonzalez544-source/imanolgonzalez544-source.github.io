Senior Design Capstone: Autogyro

This project focused on developing a 3D-printed autogyro through CAD design, weight reduction, and design iteration. I created detailed SolidWorks models for the canopy, fuselage, and airfoil geometries, while helping optimize the design for lighter weight and better manufacturability.

My Role

I worked on CAD development, part optimization, and design communication. A major contribution was helping achieve a 32% reduction in total component weight through optimized print settings and material selection.
